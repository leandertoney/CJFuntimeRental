Why Rent the Can-Am Spyder?

This is for the touring rider. The Spyder is stable, comfortable, and loaded with features. Two wheels in front provide incredible stability in corners. Tandem seating like a motorcycle means you and a passenger ride one behind the other.

Perfect for: Experienced riders with motorcycle licenses, touring enthusiasts, couples who want tandem seating, riders seeking ultimate stability and comfort.

What Makes It Special

The Can-Am Spyder F3 Limited is the premium touring option. With heated grips, Garmin GPS, and the Y-architecture stability system, it's built for long rides through Pennsylvania Dutch Country. The semi-automatic transmission means no clutch — just shift and go.
