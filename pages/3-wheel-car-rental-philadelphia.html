Best 3-Wheel Rides from the Philadelphia Area

Pick up in Lancaster or arrange a meetup, then hit some of the best roads in the mid-Atlantic:

  • Brandywine Valley Scenic Byway — Roll through Chadds Ford, Kennett Square, and the rolling hills of Chester County. Horse farms, stone bridges, and almost no traffic. A natural fit for a Slingshot.
  • Route 30 through Amish Country — The classic Philadelphia-to-Lancaster drive, completely transformed on a Slingshot. Pick up in Lancaster and make the entire corridor your playground.
  • Valley Forge to Lancaster Loop — Head west from Valley Forge on PA-23 through Phoenixville and Morgantown into Lancaster County. Excellent back roads, covered bridges, and farm stands the whole way.
  • New Hope & Delaware River Road — Head north from Philadelphia to New Hope on River Road (PA-32). One of the most dramatic river drives in the Northeast — narrow, winding, cliff-side views.
  • Lancaster to Gettysburg — Pick up in Lancaster, drive west on US-30 to Gettysburg, ride the 24-mile battlefield auto tour, and head back. A full-day 3-wheel adventure from the Philly area.
