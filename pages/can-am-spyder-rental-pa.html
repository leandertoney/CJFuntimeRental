Best Can-Am Spyder Routes from Lancaster, PA

The Spyder's touring setup — Garmin GPS, heated grips, comfortable passenger seating — makes it ideal for longer rides. These are the routes renters love most:

  • Lancaster to the Pocono Mountains — 90 minutes north via I-78. Take the Spyder up into the Poconos for mountain switchbacks, lake overlooks, and open ridge roads. Best done on a weekday to avoid tourist traffic.
  • Gettysburg Day Trip — 45 minutes west from Lancaster. Ride the 24-mile Gettysburg Battlefield loop road. The Spyder's relaxed seating position makes this a perfect full-day pair-ride.
  • Lancaster to Philadelphia — 90 minutes east on US-30 or I-76. Take the scenic route through Downingtown and Malvern. Destination ride for renters who want to make a day of it.
  • Amish Country Scenic Loop — Route 340 through Bird-in-Hand, Intercourse, and Strasburg. 45 miles of flat farmland and back roads from Lancaster — a perfect half-day intro ride for first-timers on the Spyder.
  • Susquehanna River Road — PA-441 north from Columbia to Marietta. River views the whole way, light traffic, and easy parking at small river towns. 40-mile round trip from Lancaster.
