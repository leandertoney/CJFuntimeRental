Best Rides from Hershey on a Slingshot

Pick up in Lancaster and make the most of your time in Central PA:

  • Hershey to Amish Country Loop — Head east from Hershey on PA-743 into Lancaster County, then wind through Bird-in-Hand, Intercourse, and Strasburg on Route 340. A 60–80 mile loop through some of the most photographed farmland in America.
  • Susquehanna River Road (PA-441) — From Lancaster, head north along the Susquehanna to Marietta and Elizabethtown. Then cut across to Hershey on PA-230. A perfect loop that shows you both sides of the region.
  • Dauphin County Backroads — From Hershey, head north into Dauphin County on PA-225 toward Halifax. Forested ridge roads with long views over the valley — almost no traffic, great pavement.
  • Gettysburg from Hershey — Head southwest on US-15 to Gettysburg, 50 minutes from Hershey. Ride the 24-mile battlefield auto tour, grab lunch in town, and head back via US-30 through Lancaster.
  • Covered Bridge Scenic Byway — Head to Lancaster and wind through the southern county's 28 historic covered wooden bridges. One of the most unique drives in Pennsylvania, unforgettable on a Slingshot.
