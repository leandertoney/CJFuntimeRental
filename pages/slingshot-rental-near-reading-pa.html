Best Rides from Reading on a Slingshot

Pick up in Lancaster and these routes are waiting for you:

  • Reading to Lancaster Scenic Loop — Take PA-272 south through Adamstown and Ephrata into Lancaster. Farm stands, antique shops, and wide-open roads the whole way. Then loop back through Lititz or Manheim for a full half-day ride.
  • Covered Bridge Scenic Byway — Lancaster County has more covered bridges than any other county in Pennsylvania. The official byway runs through the eastern county past Churchtown and Narvon — close to Reading and incredibly scenic.
  • Kutztown to Lititz — Head west from Reading through Kutztown, into Lancaster County, and down to Lititz — Pennsylvania's "coolest small town." Brick streets, craft breweries, pretzel factory. 60-mile loop total.
  • Gettysburg Day Trip — From Lancaster, it's 45 minutes to Gettysburg. Reading renters often make this a full-day trip: pick up the Slingshot in the morning, ride to Gettysburg for the afternoon, return by evening.
  • Blue Mountain Overlook Run — Head north from Lancaster toward Blue Mountain for ridge-line views. Exit onto small state routes through Lickdale and Pine Grove for a rolling, scenic mountain ride.
