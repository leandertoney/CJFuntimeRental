Best Rides from Wilmington on a Slingshot

Pick up in Lancaster and you're immediately in range of some of Pennsylvania's best roads:

  • Brandywine Valley to Lancaster — The drive from Wilmington through Kennett Square and Coatesville into Lancaster County is stunning. Rolling Chester County hills, horse farms, and stone bridges — all on a Slingshot.
  • Amish Country Loop — Route 340 — From your Lancaster pickup, head east through Bird-in-Hand, Intercourse, and Strasburg. The quintessential Central PA Slingshot experience.
  • Susquehanna River Road (PA-441) — Head north along the Susquehanna from Lancaster to Marietta and Elizabethtown. Cliff-side river views, minimal traffic, pure road.
  • Gettysburg Battlefield — 45 minutes west of Lancaster. Ride the 24-mile auto tour through the historic battlefield. A one-of-a-kind experience from the Delaware / Lancaster corridor.
  • Covered Bridge Scenic Byway — Lancaster County has 28 covered wooden bridges. Loop through the southern county via Strasburg, Pequea, and Martic on this classic back-road route.
