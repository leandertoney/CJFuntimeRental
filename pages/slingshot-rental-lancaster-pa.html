Scenic Routes from Lancaster, PA

Lancaster puts you at the center of some of Pennsylvania's best riding roads. Here are the top routes our renters take:

  • Amish Country Loop — Route 340 through Bird-in-Hand, Intercourse, and Strasburg. 45 miles of flat farmland, roadside markets, and zero traffic lights.
  • Susquehanna River Road — Head north on PA-441 along the river from Columbia to Marietta. One of Lancaster County's most underrated drives.
  • Gettysburg Day Trip — 45 minutes west on US-30. Ride the Gettysburg Battlefield Loop and stop at the National Military Park. A full day easily.
  • Covered Bridge Scenic Byway — Lancaster County has more covered bridges than any other county in PA. The official byway runs through Churchtown and Narvon.
  • Pocono Mountains — 90 minutes north via I-78. Take the Slingshot up into the Poconos for mountain roads and lake views. Best on weekdays to avoid traffic.
